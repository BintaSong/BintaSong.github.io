Hi, I'm a research fellow from National University of Singapore. My research interests include applied cryptography, especially on searchable encryption and practical secure computation.

Previously, I was a Ph.D. student at Shandong University, where I was advised by Prof. Qiuliang Xu. Before that, I completed my bachelor's degree at Harbin Engineering University.
